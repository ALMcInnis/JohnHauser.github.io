ABOUT ME

For fifteen years, I've created award-winning graphics and innovative user interfaces that have caught the notice of major brands throughout the globe. My work has been featured in design blogs throughout the world. My key strengths are visual and interactive design, with a focus on creating visuals that impress users. Currently, I work as a freelance designer, and I would love to work on your next project, be it a new website or a new logo.
